Zakiya's Favorite Disney Movies

Below you can find my top 3 favorite movies that each reflect a different stage in my life.

Princess and the Frog: My favorite childhood Disney movie

This is one of my favorite movies from my chikldhood because it inspired me to follow my dreams, and appreciate life's journey even as it took strange turns.

Inside Out: My favorite teenage Disney movie

This movie later inspired the name of my business and also made me feel seen as it portrayed a visual aspect of what was happening inside of my teenage brain.

Encanto: My favorite young adult Disney movie so far

This movie resonated with me because it taught me to appreciate what makes me different even when it's not yet visible to the naked eye yet.
